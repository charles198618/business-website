ABOUT US

At American Home Warranty Company (AHWC) our mission is simple - provide superior warranty service and coverage to homeowners, now and in the future. AHWC always keeps your best interest in mind, today and every day.

I am proud to say that we have been successful in that mission since 2002. Even through the housing market crash in 2009, our business has persevered. We continue to help inspectors grow their businesses while also providing limited coverage to homeowners every single day.

To our home inspectors - thank you for trusting us with your business. We value your partnership as we fight to change the home warranty business for the better.

To our homeowners - we appreciate the opportunity to participate in your home buying experience. We hope to do our part in leaving you happy in your new home.

Brittany Baldwin, President and CEO
