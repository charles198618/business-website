OUR SERVICES

American Home Warranty Company provides a low-cost home warranty for mechanical and structural components
inspected during full home inspections.

COVERAGE ON MECHANICAL / STRUCTURAL COMPONENTS INSPECTED AND IN GOOD WORKING ORDER* INCLUDE:

  • Plumbing - Water lines. Drain lines. Gas lines. Faucets. Water heaters
  • Electrical - service panel. Secondary service panels. Wiring
  • Built-in Appliances - Oven. Range. Dishwasher. Microwave Garbage disposal
  • Climate Control - All Central Heating and Air Conditioning Units
  • Foundation - Poured concrete and block wall foundations.
  • Framework - Floor joists. Structural framing.
  • Roofing - All roof coverings (limited to repair of leaking area only).
  • Walls - Exterior and/or load bearing walls, interior wall framing.
  • Garage Doors - Attached garage vehicle doors.
